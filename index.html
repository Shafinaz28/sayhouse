FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do SayHomes Team Assist in Govt Approval?

Yes.

2. Which are the services provided by SayHomes?

Building Construction | Architecture | Interior Design | Consultation

3. How area is considered?

Slab (roof to roof) area is considered.

4. What does Package includes?

All costs for construction including design & execution. The cost also includes finishing items like sanitary fixtures, doors & windows. Go through our packages here for a better understanding.

5. Who will monitor Site?

By dedicated Civil site Engineer or Execution Manager.

6. Does the package inclusive of tax amount?

Yes, Tax are inclusive.

7. Does Retention amt is added in Payment Schedule?

2% on Total project cost is considered as retention amount and paid After handover.

8. Can Client Customize Packages?

Yes. Upgradation of packages will be charged extra.
