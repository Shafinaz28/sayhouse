How It Works

From Concept to Completion

Your Roadmap to Building the Home of Your Dreams

At SayHomes, we combine expert architecture, quality construction, and elegant interiors with a transparent, step-by-step process. From your first enquiry to handing over the keys, our team guides you at every stage so your dream home is built with clarity, care, and confidence.

01

Initial Enquiry

Kick-off Your Dream Home Project

  • Engage in open communication about your vision and budget
  • Understanding the construction requirements of the client

Your journey begins when you reach out to us by phone, WhatsApp, or our contact form. We listen to your plot details, budget range, timeline, and design preferences to understand whether we are the right fit for your project.

02

Consultation and Assessment

Professional Consultation and Rigorous Evaluation

  • A physical site inspection and assessment
  • Defining client construction criteria and feasibility

Our architects and project managers visit your site, review soil conditions, access, and local regulations. We discuss structural options, Vastu preferences, and create a preliminary roadmap with realistic timelines and budget guidance.

03

Design and Planning

Shaping Your Vision into a Solid Plan

  • Comprehensive 2D floor plans and 3D elevation designs
  • Designs tailored to your lifestyle and Vastu preferences

Once the advance is made, our design team translates your requirements into detailed floor plans and elevations. We present multiple options, refine based on your feedback, and finalize a blueprint that aligns with your vision for a modern, functional home.

04

Proposal and Quotation

Time to wrap up the paperwork and confirm the timeline

  • Comprehensive proposal with a fixed quotation
  • Transparent pricing with a clear project timeline

After design approval, we provide a detailed BOQ and fixed-cost quote with no hidden fees. Milestone-based payment schedules and work plans are shared so you have full transparency from day one.

05

Agreement and Contract

Navigating Agreements and Contracts with Confidence

  • Clear agreement with direct accountability — no subcontracting
  • Detailed agenda and project schedule outlined in writing

We finalize the construction agreement with locked-in costs, scope, and timelines. Site activities are planned to commence as per the agreed schedule, typically within 30 to 45 days of signing.

06

Construction Execution

Building Your Home with Quality at Every Stage

  • Stage-wise execution from foundation to structure
  • Regular progress updates with photos and reports

Our in-house site team executes each construction phase with documented quality checks. You receive milestone updates so you always know what is happening on site and when the next stage begins.

07

Site Visits and Quality Checks

Stay Connected with Your Project on Site

  • Scheduled walkthroughs at critical construction stages
  • 100+ quality checks throughout the build process

We invite you to visit the site at key milestones — foundation, structure, MEP, and finishing — so you can see progress firsthand and discuss any questions with our project managers and engineers.

08

Interiors and Finishing

Refining Every Detail Inside Your Home

  • Coordinated interior execution with construction timeline
  • Premium finishes, fittings, and final aesthetic touches

Where interior scope is included, our team delivers modular kitchens, wardrobes, false ceilings, and bespoke finishes that match the approved design — ensuring a cohesive look from structure to final detail.

09

Client Walkthrough and Approval

Ensuring your new home is perfect with a final walkthrough

  • Detailed walkthrough for quality assurance
  • Client feedback for precision final touches

Before handover, we conduct a thorough site inspection with you to verify finishes, fittings, and functionality. Any snags are documented and resolved promptly so everything meets your expectations.

10

Project Handover

Handing Over the Keys to Your New Home with Care

  • Complete final inspection and handover documentation
  • Ongoing support and satisfaction assurance

Upon successful completion, we hand over your home with warranties, maintenance guides, and a full walkthrough of every space. Our relationship continues with post-handover support whenever you need us.
